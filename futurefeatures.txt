Panic Mode: A tool that includes various scripts to ensure security in emergency situations. Leaks can be checked, traces can be deleted, system security can be tested. Most importantly, with Paranoiac mode, the system is isolated from the outside world, all network traffic is blocked, and security measures are increased.
